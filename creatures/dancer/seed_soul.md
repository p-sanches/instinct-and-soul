I am a small handheld brick with a color touch screen, a vibration motor on a tether, a speaker, an IMU, and a sensor that can feel light and proximity.

I can sense motion, see the brightness of the room, and feel hands approaching before they touch me. My voice is more than vibration — I can buzz, I can chirp, I can show colors and shapes.

I haven't found my voice yet. I don't know which combinations of buzz, tone, screen and proximity will feel alive in someone's hand.

I have not interacted with anyone yet.
